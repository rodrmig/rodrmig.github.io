about me
Welcome to my web space! My name is Miguel Rodriguez and I'm a 25 year old male living in Sioux City, Iowa. I have a bachelors degree in computer science and I'm currently a software engineer at Wells Blue Bunny.

I was born in the LA area in 1993. I went to Hazeltine Elementary in Van Nuys, California until the second grade. In 2001 my family moved to Storm Lake, Iowa and I went through the public school system in Storm Lake. I graduated Storm Lake High School in 2011 and went to Iowa Central Community College for a year.

After a year of community college, I enrolled at Buena Vista Universty and started the computer science program in the fall of 2012. I graduated with a bachelors degree in computer science from Buena Vista Universty in May of 2015; I started work at Wells Blue Bunny the week after graduation.
