about me
Welcome to my (web) space! My name is Miguel Rodriguez and I am a 25 year old male living in Sioux City, Iowa. I am currently a programmer analyst at Wells Blue Bunny, the third largest ice cream maker in the United States.

I was born in the Los Angeles area but moved to Storm Lake, Iowa when I was eight years old. I went through the public school system in Storm Lake and enrolled at Buena Vista University, a small private college, after graduating high school. I graduated from Buena Vista University in 2015 with a bachelors degree in computer science.

I’ve envisioned myself working with computers since I was a kid. I got my first computer when I was in eight grade, and I fell in love with it. I’ve always been a logical thinker and math has always come easy to me. When I was introduced to programming in college, it was easy for me to learn since it was all logic. I’m constantly learning and getting better at my craft … I don’t know everything, but I can learn anything.
